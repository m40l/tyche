About Tyche

What is Tyche

You and your friends own a ton of games. You want to play something together, but there are too many games to choose from! And everyone has a different favorite! Enter Tyche.

Key Terms

  • Session: Put everyone who's playing in a session, and sync your common games to generate a list of all the games that everyone owns.
  • Custom Games: Tyche can't know everything you and your friends might want to do! Maybe you all would be open to buying a game if it was rolled. Maybe you want to play something not on a platform like skribbl.io. Maybe you and your friends eventually want to have a watch party for that movie you always reference. Whatever it is, you can add as a custom game.
  • Bans: Bans will never be chosen. Not every game is fun every time. Make sure you all agree on what's banned and what isn't before you click Choose Game! (or at least most of you agree)
